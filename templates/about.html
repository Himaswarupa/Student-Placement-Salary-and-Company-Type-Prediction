About Student Placement Prediction

The Student Placement Prediction System is an innovative tool designed to help students understand their placement prospects based on their academic performance and skills. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, our system analyzes various factors that influence placement outcomes.

How It Helps Students

  • Provides early insights into placement possibilities
  • Helps identify areas for improvement
  • Offers salary predictions for better career planning
  • Suggests potential company types based on profile
  • Enables better preparation for placement season

Our system considers multiple factors including CGPA, technical skills, projects, internships, and communication skills to provide accurate predictions. This helps students focus their efforts on areas that need improvement and make informed decisions about their career preparation.
